Sales & Marketing

Considered sales campaigns built to protect your result.

A sale is the sum of many decisions - positioning, presentation, buyer management, and disciplined negotiation. Matte Black runs each of them deliberately, with the strategy and judgement to protect your price.

Strategy and discipline behind every decision.

Strategy

Positioning & price guidance

We anchor your guide in genuine comparable evidence, then position the campaign to reach the buyers most likely to compete - so the market forms a view of value on your terms.

Campaign

Presentation & marketing

Photography, copywriting, and a considered launch present the home at its best across the channels that matter, building momentum through the opening weeks of the campaign.

Negotiation

Discipline that protects price

We recommend a focused expression-of-interest campaign and manage enquiry, inspections, and offers with discipline - holding the line through negotiation rather than rushing to the first number.
